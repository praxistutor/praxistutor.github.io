Second Lens

Second Lens is a reflective reasoning companion available on the GPT Store. It helps users clarify assumptions, explore alternative frames, identify blind spots, and make more informed decisions.

What Second Lens Does

  • Surfaces assumptions and hidden constraints
  • Identifies risks, blind spots, and alternative perspectives
  • Encourages safe and powerful use of LLMs
  • Supports learning, academic reasoning, and decision framing

What Second Lens Is Not

  • Not a therapist or emotional support tool
  • Not a source of professional, legal, financial, or medical advice
  • Not a productivity bot or homework substitute
